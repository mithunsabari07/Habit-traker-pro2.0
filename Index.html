About HabitTracker Pro

HabitTracker Pro is a sophisticated habit-tracking application designed to help individuals build and maintain positive daily routines. Our platform combines intuitive design with powerful analytics to make habit formation easier and more engaging.

Key Features:

  • Track unlimited habits with customizable goals
  • Visual analytics with daily, weekly, and monthly reports
  • Progress tracking with insightful charts and graphs
  • Data privacy - all data stored locally in your browser
  • Export/import functionality for data backup
  • Responsive design for all devices

Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

HabitTracker Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's how we handle your data:

Data Storage: All your habit data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Data Ownership: You own 100% of your data. You can export it anytime as a JSON file and delete it completely by using the "Reset All Data" button.

Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies or analytics cookies. The application uses only essential browser storage for functionality.

Third Parties: We don't share data with any third parties because we don't collect any data.

Changes to Policy: Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the application.
